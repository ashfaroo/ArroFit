Our mission

"Every student deserves the same quality of college advising — regardless of where they go to school or what they can afford."

Private college counselors can cost thousands of dollars. School counselors are often stretched across hundreds of students. The result is that the quality of your college list often depends on your zip code — and that's not fair.

Arrofit was built to change that. By combining the knowledge of experienced admissions counselors with the power of modern AI, we give every high school and college student access to the same quality of guidance — personalized, thoughtful, and completely free.

We believe the right college fit can change the trajectory of your life. We're here to help you find it.

— The Arrofit Team
