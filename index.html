AI Disclaimer

Last updated: May 2025

Arrofit AI uses artificial intelligence to generate personalized college list recommendations. Please read the following carefully before relying on any information provided by this tool.

AI-generated recommendations

The college list generated by Arrofit AI is produced by Claude, an AI model developed by Anthropic. The recommendations are based on patterns in training data and the information you provide. They are not produced by a licensed college counselor, admissions professional, or academic advisor.

Limitations of AI

AI models can make mistakes. The recommendations you receive may not account for:

  • Recent changes in college admissions policies or acceptance rates
  • Your full personal circumstances and background
  • Nuances that an experienced human counselor would consider
  • Real-time availability of programs or financial aid

Data accuracy

Acceptance rates and tuition figures are sourced from the US Department of Education College Scorecard and may not reflect the most current admissions cycle. Always verify data directly with colleges.

Use as a starting point

We recommend using Arrofit AI as a starting point for your college search — not as a final decision-making tool. Supplement this list with research, campus visits, conversations with current students, and guidance from a school counselor.

No guarantee of admission

Being placed on a "match" or "safety" list does not guarantee admission to any institution. College admissions decisions are made by each institution independently and depend on many factors beyond academic statistics.
